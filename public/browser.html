Four Sandboxes

Uncompromising security that never gets in your way.

🔒

Page Sandbox

Every page isolated until you prove you're actually using it. 3 meaningful interactions unlock permissions. Anti-clickjacking (20x20px minimum), anti-bot (1 second spacing), ad container detection built in.

🚫

Smart Popup Blocker

Blocks garbage. Allows Google OAuth, CAPTCHAs, and payment processors automatically. Rate limiting (5 per 30 seconds), size validation, page-load blocking. Smart enough to know the difference.

📦

Download Quarantine

Downloads don't touch your filesystem until you approve them. Multiple confirmations + 5-second delay. Heuristic scanning for suspicious patterns. Your kid can't accidentally run malware.

📡

Network Monitor

Always-visible activity indicator. Hover to see every active connection: what's connecting, what's downloading, bytes per second. No mystery traffic. No hidden requests.

🔐

Password Vault

Built-in password manager encrypted with your key. ChaCha20-Poly1305 encryption, Argon2id for PIN protection. Syncs to phone via Tailscale. Replaces LastPass, 1Password, Chrome passwords.

📱

Phone Sync

Your desktop and phone talk directly via Tailscale mesh. No cloud servers. No accounts on our systems. End-to-end encrypted. Works on home WiFi or across the planet.

⏱️

Smart History

History doesn't save for 14.7 seconds. Click a bad link? Hit back. Gone. Stay on a page? It's saved. Your history becomes intentional. NSFW auto-detected and excluded from history and sync.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Profiles

Adult, Teen, and Kid profiles with different permission levels. Kids need parent approval for downloads. Time limits. Site allowlists. Weekly reports. Finally, parental controls that work.

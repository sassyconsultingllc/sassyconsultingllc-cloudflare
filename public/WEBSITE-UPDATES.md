## SECURITY SECTION

### The 4-Layer Sandbox

**Layer 1: Network Sandbox**
All downloaded content stays in memory. No direct filesystem access.

**Layer 2: Render Sandbox**  
Our SassyScript engine has no JIT compiler = no JIT exploits. No V8 vulnerabilities.

**Layer 3: Content Sandbox**
Images and fonts decoded in pure Rust. No native codec vulnerabilities.

**Layer 4: Download Quarantine**
Files require 3 deliberate actions + 5 second wait to release. Your grandma literally cannot accidentally install malware.

### Trust Progression
Pages start untrusted. After 3 meaningful interactions (scroll, click, type), they earn trust. Malicious pages can't auto-click their way to trust.

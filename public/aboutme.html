About Shane

Built by hand. Forged in service. Driven by purpose.

Since Childhood

Roots — Growing Up Building

I grew up in a family that built custom homes. Zach Builders was the family business, and the job site was my classroom. Before I understood blueprints, I understood work — showing up early, doing the job right, taking pride in something you built with your own hands.

Framing walls, pouring foundations, finishing trim — I learned that everything worth having starts from the ground up. That stuck with me.

Zach Builders
Growing Up

Side by Side — Mom's Cleaning Company

My mom ran a cleaning company, and her primary client was the building company. So while the crew built the homes, we came behind them and made them shine. I was right there beside her, learning what it means to finish the job — not just do it, but do it so well nobody questions it.

She taught me that the work nobody sees is the work that matters most. Attention to detail. Showing up when you said you would. Taking care of people's spaces like they were your own. I carry that with me in everything I build today.

2010 – 2014

College — Eau Claire, WI

Headed to Eau Claire, Wisconsin for college. Balanced classes with the work ethic that was already second nature — the kind you pick up when you've been on job sites and cleaning crews since you were a kid.

11 Years

Service — U.S. Army

I served 11 years in the United States Army as a Chemical Warfare Officer. Chemical Warfare isn't about using weapons — it's about understanding threats invisible to the naked eye. Protecting people from dangers they can't see coming. Building systems that keep everyone safe, even when they don't know they're in danger.

That mission shaped everything I do now. The threats changed — from chemical agents to malware, from battlefields to browsers — but the job is the same: protect people who didn't sign up for this fight.

Recent

Helping Others Build — Riverview Adventure Company

Built the website for Cory's Riverview Adventure Company. Same principle as always — someone needs something built right, you show up and build it. Whether it's a house, a clean space, or a website, the craft is the same.

Riverview Adventure Company
Now

Building Again — Sassy Consulting LLC

Everything I learned — building homes with my family, cleaning and finishing alongside my mom, protecting people in the Army — it all led here. Sassy Consulting LLC is a veteran-owned software company in Wisconsin, and I build privacy-focused tools that put control back in people's hands.

No tracking. No telemetry. No data harvesting. Just tools that work, built by someone who knows what it means to protect people and take pride in the craft. Still serving. Different uniform.

Contact: info@sassyconsultingllc.com

2. What SassyTalk Does

SassyTalk is a push-to-talk voice communication app that works like a digital walkie-talkie. Users press a button to transmit voice to other devices on the same encrypted channel. Audio is encrypted with AES-256-GCM on your device before transmission and can only be decrypted by devices that share your session key.

The app supports two communication paths:

  • WiFi Multicast — direct device-to-device on the same local network. No server involved.
  • Cellular Relay — encrypted audio forwarded through a Cloudflare relay server at relay.sassy-consults.com. The relay is a blind binary forwarder and cannot decrypt your audio.

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Voice recordings (audio is never stored on any server)
  • Transcription text (Whisper speech-to-text runs entirely on your device and never leaves it)
  • Email addresses, phone numbers, or any account information (no accounts exist)
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics or behavioral tracking data

3.3 Data Shared With Other Users in Your Session

The following is transmitted to other devices in your session as part of the audio wire frame:

  • Display name and emoji avatar — set by you in the Profile screen. Transmitted in the wire frame header (unencrypted) so peers can identify who is speaking.
  • Device sender ID — a random identifier generated per session. Transmitted in the wire frame header (unencrypted).
  • Encrypted audio — AES-256-GCM ciphertext. Only devices with the session key can decrypt this.
  • Channel and subchannel numbers — transmitted unencrypted for routing. Does not reveal audio content.

3.4 Data Stored On Your Device

  • Session keys — stored in Android SharedPreferences. These are the AES-256-GCM encryption keys for your active channels. Clearing app data removes them.
  • Profile — your chosen display name, emoji, and color. Stored in SharedPreferences.
  • Whisper model — a 75MB speech recognition model file (ggml-tiny.en.bin) downloaded from HuggingFace and stored on device for on-device transcription.
  • Temporary audio cache — recent audio is held in memory for replay. Cleared when the app closes. Never written to disk.

5. Third-Party Services

Cloudflare (Infrastructure)

Our relay server runs on Cloudflare Workers and Durable Objects. Cloudflare processes connection metadata (IP addresses, timestamps, request counts) as part of their infrastructure. We do not have access to Cloudflare's infrastructure logs. Cloudflare's privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

HuggingFace (Model Download)

The Whisper speech recognition model is downloaded from HuggingFace's public model repository. This is a one-time download. HuggingFace may log the download request (IP, timestamp). No audio or personal data is sent to HuggingFace. HuggingFace's privacy policy: huggingface.co/privacy

No Other Third Parties

SassyTalk does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or social media integrations.

6. Data Security

  • End-to-end encryption: All voice audio is encrypted with AES-256-GCM before leaving your device. The encryption key is generated locally and shared only via QR code or manual code entry.
  • Blind relay: The relay server forwards encrypted binary packets. It does not have the encryption key and cannot decrypt audio content.
  • No server storage: Audio packets are forwarded in real-time and immediately discarded. There is no database, no recording, no replay capability on any server.
  • Screen protection: The app blocks screenshots, screen recording, and app preview in the recent apps list.
  • Local transcription: Speech-to-text runs entirely on your device using the Whisper model. No audio is sent to any external transcription service.

9. Data Retention

  • Audio: Never stored. Forwarded in real-time and discarded.
  • Transcription: Exists only in device memory while the app is running. Not written to disk.
  • Session keys: Stored on your device until you clear app data or the session expires.
  • Cloudflare logs: Retained per Cloudflare's data retention policies. We do not access or control these logs.
  • On-device profile: Stored until you clear app data or uninstall the app.

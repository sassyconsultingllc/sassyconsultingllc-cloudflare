Privacy Policy

Cloudflare Admin — infrastructure management app for Cloudflare accounts

Package: com.cloudflare.sassyconsults.app (Android) / com.cfadmin.mobile (iOS)

Effective Date: July 1, 2026   Last Updated: July 1, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Sassy Consulting, LLC ("Company," "we," "us," "our") handles information in connection with the Cloudflare Admin mobile and desktop application ("the App"), available on Android, iOS, desktop, and web. We are a Cloudflare infrastructure management tool built with privacy as a design constraint, not an afterthought: the App holds no accounts with us, and virtually everything it touches stays on your device.

This policy is written to comply with data protection laws worldwide, including the EU/EEA General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), UK GDPR, the California Consumer Privacy Act as amended by the CPRA, other U.S. state privacy laws, Canada's PIPEDA, Brazil's LGPD, Australia's Privacy Act 1988, South Africa's POPIA, Singapore's PDPA, and India's DPDPA.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Cloudflare API Token. You generate this yourself in the Cloudflare dashboard and enter it into the App to authenticate API calls. This is the only credential required to use the App.
  • Optional Google Gemini API Key. If you choose Google Gemini as your AI provider instead of the default (Cloudflare Workers AI), you supply your own free API key from Google AI Studio.
  • Notification Preferences & Theme Settings. Local toggles you set in the App (e.g., which alert channels are enabled).

Information Generated by Your Use of the App

  • AI Prompts and, Optionally, On-Screen Screenshots. If you invoke the AI assistant sidebar and choose to include a screenshot of the current screen, that prompt text and image are sent directly from your device to whichever AI provider you have selected (see "AI Features" below). This is opt-in per message — it is not automatic, and it is not available on all platforms.
  • Local Session Change Log. The App keeps a running, on-device summary of actions you take (e.g., "purged cache for zone X") so you can review or export it. This log never leaves your device unless you manually export it.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, physical address, or government ID.
  • We do not operate a login/account system. There is no "Sassy Consulting account" tied to your use of the App.
  • We do not use analytics, crash-reporting, or telemetry SDKs of any kind (no Firebase, Google Analytics, Sentry, Amplitude, Mixpanel, or similar). We do not know how often you open the App, what screens you view, or how you use it.
  • We do not use advertising and do not share data with ad networks.
  • We do not access your device's camera, microphone, contacts, precise location, or files.

How Your Cloudflare API Token Is Used

Your Cloudflare API token authenticates requests the App makes directly from your device to Cloudflare's official API (api.cloudflare.com) to read and manage the zones, DNS records, Workers, R2 buckets, KV namespaces, D1 databases, Hyperdrive configs, and security/analytics data your token is scoped to access. The token is stored only on your device (see "Data Storage" below) and is never transmitted to any server operated by us.

Optional AI Features

The App includes an optional AI assistant ("AI sidebar" / "AI Edit") that can answer questions, review Worker code, and suggest edits. You choose which AI provider powers it:

  • Cloudflare Workers AI (default). Runs the llama-3.2-11b-vision-instruct model via your own Cloudflare account and API token. Requests go to api.cloudflare.com. Governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini (optional). Runs Gemini 2.0 Flash using your own Google AI Studio API key. Requests go to generativelanguage.googleapis.com. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy and the Gemini API Additional Terms of Service.

Screenshots. On desktop and web builds, you may optionally attach a screenshot of the app UI to your message to the AI assistant (the assistant's own sidebar and floating button are excluded from the capture). If you do, that image — which may show Cloudflare account data visible on screen at that moment — is sent to whichever provider above you have selected, together with your text prompt. This never happens automatically; it only happens when you actively choose to include a screenshot. This feature is not available on native mobile (Android/iOS) builds.

We do not operate our own AI models and do not receive a copy of your prompts, screenshots, or AI responses. They travel directly from your device to Cloudflare's or Google's servers, using your own credentials.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

To the extent any processing of personal data occurs, our legal bases are:

  • Consent: You choose to enter your API token, choose an AI provider, and choose whether to attach a screenshot.
  • Contractual necessity: Processing needed to provide the App's core functionality you requested.
  • Legitimate interest: Keeping the App secure and functioning as described.

Data Storage & Retention

All App data lives on your device:

  • Encrypted secure storage. Your Cloudflare API token, any Gemini API key, and session preferences are stored using the platform's native secure storage (Android Keystore via Expo SecureStore; iOS Keychain).
  • Local app storage. Notification preferences, theme, and cached UI state are stored in local app storage (e.g., AsyncStorage), not on any server.
  • No server-side storage. We do not operate a backend that stores your API token, your Cloudflare account data, your AI chat history, or your session change log. What you see in the App is fetched live from Cloudflare's API each time; nothing is cached on our infrastructure.
  • Retention. Data is retained locally until you remove an account, clear app data, or uninstall the App — at which point it is deleted from your device.

International Data Transfers

Because we do not receive or store your data on our own servers, we do not ourselves transfer your personal data internationally. When you use the App's AI features, your prompt (and optional screenshot) is transferred directly from your device to Cloudflare's or Google's global infrastructure, subject to their own cross-border transfer safeguards (including Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable). Review Cloudflare's and Google's policies for details on their international transfer mechanisms.

Third-Party Services

The App communicates with the following third-party services, and only these:

  • Cloudflare API (api.cloudflare.com) — required, for all zone/account management features.
  • Cloudflare Workers AI — used only if you invoke the AI assistant with the default provider.
  • Google Gemini API (generativelanguage.googleapis.com) — used only if you select Gemini as your AI provider and supply your own key.

No other third party, SDK, or analytics vendor receives data from the App.

Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions:

  • Internet access. Required to reach Cloudflare's and, if selected, Google's APIs over HTTPS.
  • Notifications (Android POST_NOTIFICATIONS; iOS notification permission). Optional. Used only to show local, on-device alerts (see below). You may deny or revoke this at any time in your device settings.

The App does not request camera, microphone, contacts, precise location, storage/files, or Bluetooth permissions.

Notifications

The App can generate local, on-device notifications for security events, deployment updates, zone status changes, and SSL certificate expirations — all derived from data already fetched from your Cloudflare account. These notifications are scheduled and shown entirely on-device and, as currently configured, are not routed through any push-notification server operated by us.

Data Security

  • API tokens and keys are stored using platform-native encrypted secure storage, never in plaintext.
  • All network traffic uses HTTPS/TLS.
  • The App does not log your API token or AI provider key to disk or to any diagnostic service.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is perfectly secure. You are responsible for keeping your device secure (e.g., using a device passcode/biometric lock) and for safeguarding the scopes granted to your Cloudflare API token.

Data Breach Notification

Because the App does not operate servers that store your Cloudflare credentials or account data, the practical risk surface for a Company-side breach affecting your data is minimal. In the unlikely event a breach of any Company system did affect personal data we hold (e.g., correspondence you send to our support address), we will notify affected individuals without undue delay, and applicable regulators as required by law.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or port your personal data, and to object to processing based on legitimate interests. Because we hold no server-side copy of your Cloudflare data or AI chat content, most of these rights are already satisfied by the App's local-only design — you can access, correct, or erase this information yourself at any time via the App's settings, or by uninstalling it. For anything else (e.g., support correspondence), contact us at the email below; we will respond within one month.

CCPA / CPRA (California Residents)

California consumers have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion, to correct inaccurate information, and to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information and have not done so in the preceding 12 months. We will not discriminate against you for exercising any CCPA/CPRA right. You may designate an authorized agent to submit requests on your behalf; we will verify the agent's authority before acting.

Other U.S. State Privacy Laws

Residents of Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, and other states with comprehensive privacy statutes have analogous rights to access, correct, delete, and port personal data, and to opt out of targeted advertising, sale, or profiling. We do not engage in targeted advertising, sale, or profiling of App users, so these opt-outs are, in practice, already the default.

LGPD (Brazil)

POPIA (South Africa)

PDPA (Singapore)

Singapore users may withdraw consent to any processing of personal data, and may request access to and correction of personal data we hold, subject to the Personal Data Protection Act.

India DPDPA

Indian Data Principals may request a summary of processing activities, correction and erasure of personal data, and may nominate another individual to exercise these rights on their behalf in the event of death or incapacity. Contact our Grievance Officer at the email below; we will acknowledge within 48 hours and resolve within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

The App is a professional infrastructure-management tool intended for use by adults administering their own (or their employer's) Cloudflare accounts. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction, e.g., 16 under GDPR unless a member state sets a lower age). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Data Deletion

  • Remove a single account: Use "Remove Account" in Settings to delete that account's stored API token from your device.
  • Remove all data: Uninstall the App, or clear its app data/storage via your device's OS settings.
  • Support correspondence: Contact us to request deletion of any information you've sent us directly (e.g., support emails).

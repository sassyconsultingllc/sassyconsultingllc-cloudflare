Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built for teams that need secure, local communication without the complexity.

ENC

AES-256-GCM Encryption

Military-grade encryption for all voice transmissions. Your conversations stay private, even on shared networks.

LAN

No Internet Required

Works entirely over local WiFi. No servers, no cloud, no external connections. Perfect for offline environments.

CH

99 Encrypted Channels

Create separate encrypted channels for different teams. Each channel has its own encryption key.

0

Zero Data Collection

No accounts, no tracking, no analytics. We never see your data because it never leaves your network.

KEY

ECDH Key Exchange

Secure key exchange using X25519 elliptic curve. Same algorithm used by Signal, WhatsApp, and WireGuard.

PTT

Push-to-Talk

Simple, familiar interface. Hold to talk, release to listen. Works exactly like a traditional walkie-talkie.

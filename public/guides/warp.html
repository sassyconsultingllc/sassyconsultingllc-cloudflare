🌐 Cloudflare WARP Setup Guide

WARP is a free VPN from Cloudflare that's fast, simple, and respects your privacy.

1

Download 1.1.1.1 App

Visit 1.1.1.1 and download the app for your device.

2

Install & Open

Run the installer and open the 1.1.1.1 app. No account needed!

3

Enable WARP

Toggle the big switch to connect. That's it - you're protected!

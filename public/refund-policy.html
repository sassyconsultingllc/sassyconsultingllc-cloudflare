Refund Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

The short version: every paid product comes with a 14-day, no-questions-asked refund. If you bought something in the last 14 days and want your money back, you get it back. That's the whole policy.

What's covered

All one-time software purchases made through this site — SassyMCP Pro, the Forensics add-on, SassyMCP Team, Sassy-Talk, WinForensics-Pro, and any product we add later. There are no subscriptions in our catalog; nothing recurs, so there's nothing to cancel.

How to request a refund

  • Email info@sassyconsultingllc.com from the address you used at checkout, or
  • Reply to your Lemon Squeezy order receipt.

Include your order number or license key if you have it handy — it speeds things up, but we can find your order by email either way. No reason required.

How it's processed

Checkout and payment are handled by Lemon Squeezy, our merchant of record. Refunds are issued to your original payment method, typically within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.

What happens to your license

When a refund is issued, the associated license key is revoked and any activated installs drop back to the free tier (or stop validating, for products without one). If you repurchase later, you'll get a fresh key.

After 14 days

Past the window, refunds are at our discretion — but if something is genuinely broken and we can't fix it for you, we'd rather refund you than keep your money. Talk to us.

Donations

Donations are gifts and aren't refundable, except where required by law or in the case of an obvious mistake (wrong amount, duplicate charge) — contact us and we'll sort it out.

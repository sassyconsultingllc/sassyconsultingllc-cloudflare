# About Me Page Design

**Date:** 2026-02-27
**Route:** `/aboutme` → `public/aboutme.html`
**Purpose:** Standalone personal story page for GoFundMe credibility

## Design Summary

Timeline-style story page with card-based chapters. Blue calm color scheme. Personal and heartfelt tone.

## Hero / Intro

- Deep navy gradient background
- Heading: "About Shane"
- Tagline: "Built by hand. Forged in service. Driven by purpose."
- Badge: "Veteran-Owned | Wisconsin"

## Timeline Cards

Vertical timeline line (thin blue accent) connecting 4 cards:

### Card 1: "Roots" — Growing Up Building
- Era: "Since childhood"
- Grew up in the Zach Builders family (zachbuilders.com), learning to build custom homes from the foundation up. Hands-on work ethic from day one.

### Card 2: "Side by Side" — Mom's Cleaning Company
- Era: "Growing up"
- Worked alongside mom in her cleaning company that serviced the building company. Learned attention to detail, pride in doing the job right, from a young age.

### Card 3: "Service" — 11 Years U.S. Army
- Era: "11 years"
- Chemical Warfare Officer. Protecting people from invisible threats. Building systems that keep everyone safe — even when they don't know they're in danger.

### Card 4: "Building Again" — Sassy Consulting LLC
- Era: "Now"
- Everything learned — building, cleaning, protecting — applied to software. Privacy-focused tools, veteran-owned, Wisconsin-made. Still serving, different uniform.
